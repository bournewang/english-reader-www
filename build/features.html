Features

Text-to-Speech

Converts article text into speech, allowing you to listen to articles while multitasking. This feature supports various languages and provides clear, natural-sounding speech to enhance your reading experience.

Bilingual Mode

Provides side-by-side translations of English articles to help improve your language skills. Easily switch between languages and understand the content better with our accurate translation feature.

Dictionary

Instantly look up word definitions and translations by double-clicking on any word. Our integrated dictionary supports multiple languages and provides detailed definitions to expand your vocabulary.
