Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how our English Reader extension collects, uses, and protects your information.

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Email addresses and passwords when users register and log in.
  • Usage Data: Information on how the extension is used, such as the articles read and words looked up.
  • Technical Data: Browser type and version, operating system, and other technical data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve the functionality of the extension.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or trade your personal information. We may share data with third parties under the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: To help us operate and improve our services, we may share information with third-party service providers.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, we may disclose your information to comply with legal processes.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information.
  • Request the deletion of your data.
  • Opt-out of data collection by uninstalling the extension.
